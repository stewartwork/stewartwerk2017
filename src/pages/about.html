HipHipDX
Freelance UI Designer | Front-End Developer

I was contracted by Cheri Media to join the team that was going to redesign, code, and move HipHopDX over to Wordpress. HipHopDx is the third largest Hip Hop site online – with 10 years worth of articles, multiple editors, and an outdated CMS. I was part of a three man team – design / front-end (me), a wordpress guru, and the Creative Director / front-end . It was a lot of work, I made some lasting friendships, and it was probably the most fun I have had working on a project.

We worked out of Ignition Alley in Atlanta which was a great time and pretty tricky. Transferring a database the size of HipHopDx would be tough with a fast connection, so working with IA's wonky wifi was a nightmare.

Since it was such a small team we all had to be multi-disciplined. The designers were coders and UX, the wordpress dev supplied UX and design. We designed infographics, rating systems, multiple landing page and article layouts.

Once the design / front-end portion of the project had been finalized I moved over to my next contract position and looked forward to the sites launch. The site took a couple of years to be updated and the full design was never actually implemented. It looks great and hopefully they will be releasing the full site design in the future.

It was a great job.
